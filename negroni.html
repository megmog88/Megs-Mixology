negroni

Negroni is an all time classic cocktail, usually had as an apertif and originally from Italy. It is a strong beverage that packs a punch and should never be served with a straw. This is actually my all time favorite cocktail. Sip Sip Sip Hooray! Now turn the cards from lowest to highest to learn how to make a negroni!

Equipment

  • Bar Spoon
  • Whiskey Glass
  • Boston Base
  • Strainer
  • Jigger(50ml)

Ingredients

  • Ice
  • Gin(SipSmith is my fave)
  • Sweet Vermouth(Martini Red)
  • Campari
  • Orange

Method

  1. Measure 50ml with your jigger of all 3 spirits(Gin, Sweet Vermouth and Campari.
  2. Add to boston base
  3. Add ice to full the base
  4. Stir with long bar spoon for about 2-3minutes
  5. Strain into whiskey glass over ice
  6. Granish zest of orange

Pairs Perfectly..

A negroni is best served at the beginning of your meal. It wets your appetite ready for a great meal. Try it with max flavour hot wing crisps, on its own or some tasty freshly picked Italian olives. Oh yum!
