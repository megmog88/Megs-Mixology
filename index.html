Cocktails

Let's start with a brief history on cocktails! There are many different theories behind why we call a cocktail a cocktail, so I decided to tell you my favorite and you can research the rest once you've finished up taking my online cocktail class. "The Dregs Theory (Cock tailings) “Cock tailings” was a term used to describe the mixture that was created when tavern owners combined the dregs (tailings) of nearly empty barrels together. The resulting mixture from several barrels would then be sold at a discounted price. This only makes sense when you know that the spigot (tap) of a barrel was sometimes referred to as a “cock.”"
